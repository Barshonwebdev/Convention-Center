Frequently Asked Questions

Event design focuses on the visual and aesthetic aspects of an event, such as décor, lighting, and ambiance, while event planning involves logistical elements like budgeting, vendor coordination, and scheduling.

It is recommended to start planning an event in a convention center at least 6-12 months in advance to secure the desired dates, coordinate logistics, and allow sufficient time for promotion and attendee preparations.

Our refund and cancellation policy states that refunds are available up to 30 days prior to the event, subject to a 20% cancellation fee. Cancellations within 30 days are non-refundable, but may be eligible for credit towards future events.

To keep budgets on track, it is essential to create a detailed budget plan, regularly track expenses against the plan, monitor variances, seek cost-saving opportunities, and maintain clear communication with stakeholders throughout the event planning process. We do all of these efficiently.
